Pro Tips
How to get the
most accurate results
☀️
Use good lighting
Natural light facing toward you works best. Avoid harsh shadows across your face — even lighting gives the model the clearest signal.
🎯
Face the camera directly
Look straight at the camera with a relaxed expression. Side angles and tilted heads reduce accuracy significantly.
🖼️
Keep a simple background
A plain, contrasting background helps the detector isolate your face cleanly. Busy backgrounds can confuse the model.
👓
Remove obstructions
Take off glasses, hats, or scarves if possible. Anything covering facial features will reduce the age and gender prediction accuracy.
